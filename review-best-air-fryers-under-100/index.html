What is an air fryer?
An air fryer is a kitchen appliance that cooks food by circulating hot air around it, producing a crispy outer layer with minimal oil. It's a healthier alternative to traditional frying methods.

How does an air fryer work?
Air fryers use a powerful fan to circulate hot air around the food, cooking it evenly from all sides. This rapid air circulation mimics the effect of deep frying while using significantly less oil.

What can you cook in an air fryer?
You can cook a wide variety of foods in an air fryer, including vegetables, meats, pastries, and even frozen foods. It's perfect for items like fries, chicken wings, and even baked goods.

Are air fryers healthy?
Yes, air fryers are considered healthier than traditional frying methods as they require less oil, which reduces calorie intake and the amount of unhealthy fats in your meals.

How do I clean an air fryer?
Cleaning an air fryer is straightforward. Most models have removable, dishwasher-safe parts. After each use, allow the appliance to cool, then wipe down the exterior and wash the basket and trays with warm, soapy water.
