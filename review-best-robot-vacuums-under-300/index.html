FAQ

What is the best robot vacuum under $300?
The MOVA S10 Robot Vacuum and Mop Combo stands out due to its high suction power and effective navigation, making it an excellent choice for various floor types.

How do I maintain my robot vacuum?
Regularly clean the brushes and filters, empty the dustbin frequently, and ensure that sensors are free of debris for optimal performance.

Can robot vacuums clean carpets effectively?
Yes, models like the MOVA S10 and iRobot Roomba 205 are designed to handle carpets effectively, especially in homes with pets.

What features should I look for in a robot vacuum?
Look for strong suction power, effective navigation, app connectivity, and versatile cleaning modes to ensure the vacuum meets your needs.

How long do robot vacuums last on a single charge?
Most robot vacuums last between 60 to 90 minutes on a single charge, but this can vary depending on the model and cleaning modes used.
