What Makes These Brands Different?

Looking at the top 20, a few patterns emerge:

  • Kitchen specialists dominate: Brands like Cuisinart, Hamilton Beach, Ninja prove that kitchen is the most competitive (and rewarding) category on Amazon.
  • Tech brands cross over: Anker, JBL, Sony appear across tech, audio, and accessories.
  • Value matters: These brands rarely compete at the luxury end. They invest in the $30-150 range where most consumers actually shop.

How We Counted

We counted each unique keyword/category where a brand appeared in our top-rated products list. A brand appearing in "best wireless earbuds" and "best noise-canceling headphones" counts as 2 appearances. We de-duplicated within each category so a brand with 3 products in one review only counts once.
