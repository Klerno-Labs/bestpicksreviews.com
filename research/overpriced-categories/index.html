What This Means for Shoppers

The data is clear: in most categories, you can get the best product for significantly less than the average price. Here's why:

  • Premium tax is real: In many categories, 2-3 luxury brands inflate the average price far above what most people need to spend.
  • The best product is rarely the most expensive: Our #1 picks are chosen for overall value, and they consistently land below the category average.
  • Research pays off: Spending 10 minutes reading reviews can save you $50-200+ per purchase.

How We Calculated This

For each of our 466 product categories, we calculated the average price of all products reviewed (3,752 total). We then compared that average to the price of our #1 ranked pick. Categories with fewer than 3 products were excluded. The "savings" figure represents the difference between the category average and the #1 pick price.
