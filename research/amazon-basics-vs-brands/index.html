When to Buy Amazon Basics

Based on our data, here's when Amazon's store brands make sense:

  • Commoditized products: Cables, basic tools, simple accessories — Amazon matches quality at lower prices.
  • Don't buy Amazon for: Anything requiring durability, precision, or taste (audio, outdoor gear, skincare). Brand expertise matters here.
  • Check the rating gap: If Amazon's product is within 0.2 stars of the brand leader, save the money. If the gap is 0.5+, pay for the brand.

How We Compared

We searched our database of 3,752 products for any Amazon-branded items (Amazon Basics, Amazon Essentials, Amazon Commercial, etc.). In each category where an Amazon brand appeared, we compared it to our #1 ranked pick on price and rating. "Amazon Wins" means Amazon matched or beat the brand on both metrics. "Brand Wins" means the name brand outperformed on rating with equal or lower price.
