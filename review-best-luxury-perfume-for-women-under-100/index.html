Buyer's Guide

Scent Longevity


When choosing a perfume, consider its longevity. A fragrance that lasts all day can provide more value for your money, especially in the luxury category. Look for perfumes that have a higher concentration of fragrance oils for longer-lasting scents.

Fragrance Notes


Understanding fragrance notes is crucial. Perfumes generally consist of top, middle, and base notes. Top notes are what you smell first, while base notes emerge later and provide depth. Choose scents that feature notes you love, whether floral, fruity, or musky.

Seasonal Wear


Some fragrances are better suited for certain seasons. Lighter, fresher scents are ideal for spring and summer, while warmer, richer fragrances are perfect for fall and winter. Consider when you'll wear the fragrance most often when making your selection.

Bottle Design


While the scent is paramount, the bottle design also plays a role. A beautifully designed bottle can make for an attractive display and can enhance the overall experience of using the fragrance. Look for options that appeal to your aesthetic tastes.

FAQ

What are the best luxury perfumes under $100?


Some of the best luxury perfumes under $100 include Merit Retrospect, Glossier You, and Phlur Afterglow. Each offers a unique scent profile and excellent longevity, making them great choices for anyone seeking affordable luxury.

How do I choose a perfume for daily wear?


To choose a daily wear perfume, look for versatile scents that suit your lifestyle and personality. Opt for lighter fragrances that are not overpowering, and consider whether you prefer floral, fruity, or musky notes.

What notes should I look for in a fragrance?


Focus on the fragrance notes that resonate with you. Floral notes are great for a fresh feel, while woody or oriental notes provide warmth and depth. Consider trying samples to see which notes you enjoy most.

How long do luxury perfumes typically last?


Luxury perfumes can last anywhere from 4 to 12 hours, depending on their concentration and your skin type. Eau de Parfum typically has a higher concentration than Eau de Toilette, resulting in longer-lasting scent.

Are celebrity perfumes worth the investment?


Celebrity perfumes can offer unique and appealing scents at competitive prices. Many are crafted with quality in mind and can be a great addition to your collection without breaking the bank.
